Category Guidelines
Revenue Categories:
  • Membership Fees: Annual membership payments
  • Event Revenue: Ticket sales, workshop fees
  • Sponsorships: Corporate sponsorships
  • Donations: Alumni and external donations
Expense Categories:
  • Equipment: Hardware, software, tools
  • Venue Costs: Room rentals, event spaces
  • Event Costs: Catering, materials, prizes
  • Marketing: Promotional materials
Important Notes:
  • Built-in Categories: Cannot be edited or deleted, but can be deactivated
  • Custom Categories: Can be edited, activated/deactivated, or deleted
  • Inactive Categories: Will not appear in transaction forms but existing transactions remain
  • Category Types: Revenue categories are for money coming in, expense categories for money going out
